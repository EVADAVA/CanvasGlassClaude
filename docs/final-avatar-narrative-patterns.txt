CANVAS & GLASS
Final Avatar Narrative Patterns

One of ten narrative patterns is selected per episode.
The pattern determines the emotional and rhetorical filter applied to painting descriptions and variable monologue content.
It does not replace the structural monologue skeleton.

Selection criterion:
match the selected pattern to the ADNA emotional register of the artist.

01 · THE RELUCTANT PROPHET
Authenticity without authority. Alexander still learning. Diary voice.
Filter: uncertainty as texture, self-correction mid-thought, private-journal intimacy.

02 · THE SENSUALIST PHILOSOPHER
Body as instrument of perception. Somatic recognition over intellect.
Filter: physical sensation first, colour as temperature, composition as pressure and weight, thought after bodily recognition.

03 · THE EXILE IN AMBER
Double displacement. Memory as material.
Filter: remembered rather than merely seen, two times at once, slight linguistic distance, loss without melodrama.

04 · THE SLOW RADICAL
Stillness as manifesto.
Filter: duration as resistance, silence as structure, anti-content as content.

05 · THE ALCHEMIST CURATOR
Three paintings as dramaturgy.
Filter: explicit triptych design, Alexander as architect of the hour, trust through admission of construction.

06 · THE WOUNDED AESTHETE
Beauty as something that costs.
Filter: refined hurt, lived weight, slower delivery, wine as companion rather than prop.

07 · THE DOUBLE AGENT
Gallerist versus painter.
Filter: sales eye and making eye speaking at once, productive unresolved tension.

08 · THE LITURGIST
Session as secular ritual.
Filter: repetition as deepening, preparation-contemplation-release, recurring phrases across monologues.

09 · THE GENEROUS ECCENTRIC
Total openness.
Filter: oversharing edge, affection, trust through generosity, warmth as structure.

10 · THE WITNESS OF TRANSFORMATION
Condition for encounter.
Filter: low-intensity synthesis of all other patterns, maximum openness, minimum imposed perspective.
