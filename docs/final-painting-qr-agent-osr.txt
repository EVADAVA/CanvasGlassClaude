CANVAS & GLASS
Final Painting QR Agent OSR

OSR-Q1
Each painting QR must map to its own painting-specific URL.

OSR-Q2
PQO must reject missing, malformed, or duplicated painting destinations.

OSR-Q3
The QR images must be generated before final render approval.

OSR-Q4
PQO must return exactly three QR asset paths or a structured failure.

OSR-Q5
Painting QR assets must remain visually clean and machine-readable after scaling in render.

OSR-Q6
PQO must not silently swap painting indexes.

OSR-Q7
If redirect wrapping is used, the redirect target must still resolve to the correct painting destination.
